Expert Advice by Varvara Kremer

People don't know the difference between cleaning eyebrows and shaping eyebrows. That is why they make such common mistakes and end up with a face expression which doesn't match their personality. For example:
  • Eyebrows too close (make your face look tired and sad)
  • Eyebrows too far apart (make the nose look wider and the eyes appear smaller.)
  • Eyebrows too bushy (make you look older)
  • Eyebrow too thin (make an unfinished look)
  • Eyebrows arched to high (make a look of surprise or anger)

Teens start to tweeze their eyebrows at the age of 12 because they want to be either "cool" or they are ashamed about their unibrow or very bushy eyebrows. At this age, it is very easy to damage roots of eyebrow hair. Unfortunely, eyebrows stop growing in those places where damaged. Help them avoid mistakes which could follow them throughout their whole lives.

Creating the perfect eyebrows isn't just about trimming and tweezing. It's about creating a look, a style, a feeling. Correctly shaped eyebrows can completely change a face, or simply improve what is already there and help bring the "true you" Do not wax your eyebrows, tweeze only if:

  • You have peeling skin
  • You have had recent laser treatment or cosmetic facial work
  • You use Accutane, Retin-A or other prescription acne medications
